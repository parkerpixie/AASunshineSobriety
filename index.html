Note

How to Use This Dashboard

SAY means read this out loud.

DO means leader directions only. Do not read out loud.

COPY TO CHAT means use the copy button and paste into Zoom chat.

NOTE means helpful reminder or tech tip.

This dashboard is built from the full SunShiners / Sunrisers 7:30 AM Meeting Leader Script.

✅
Do

Before the Meeting Starts

Have these pages open before the meeting begins:

  1. This leader dashboard.
  2. Chapter 5 - How It Works, in case someone else cannot read or you need to paste the link in chat.
  3. AA Daily Reflections, so you can copy and paste the day’s reflection link.
  4. Full Leader Script Google Doc, if you need the original script.

You do not need to claim host or ask for readers before the meeting starts. Those happen during the meeting.

💬
Say

1. Welcome

Good morning, welcome to the 7:30 am Sunrisers meeting of AA. I am ______ and I’m an Alcoholic, or however you choose to introduce yourself. Let’s begin with a moment of silence for the still suffering alcoholic, followed by the serenity prayer.

Opening Serenity Prayer, I Form
God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change,
the courage to change the things I can,
and the wisdom to know the difference.

💬
Say

2. Preamble

Leader reads the preamble.

Alcoholics Anonymous is a fellowship of people who share their experience, strength, and hope with each other that they may solve their common problem and help others to recover from alcoholism.

💬
Say

3. Newcomers, Visitors, and First Step Meeting

Is this anyone’s 1st meeting of AA?

Do we have anyone visiting our meeting who’d like to be recognized?

Would anyone like a first step meeting for any reason?

🧭
Do

If Someone Requests a First Step Meeting

If no one requests a first step meeting, continue to Chapter 5 Reading.

If someone requests a first step meeting:

  • Welcome the newcomer.
  • Point out the resource packet and contact list in the announcements.
  • Ask for a volunteer to read Chapter 3, More About Alcoholism from the Readings Appendix.
  • Skip Chapter 5 for this meeting.
💬
Say

4. Ask for Chapter 5 Reader

Now we need a volunteer to read the first part of CHAPTER 5 - HOW IT WORKS

Open Chapter 5 - How It Works

📋
Do

While Chapter 5 Is Being Read

  1. Copy and paste Announcements Part 1 into Zoom chat.
  2. Copy and paste Announcements Part 2 into Zoom chat.
  3. Open the AA Daily Reflections page so the reflection link is ready.
🖱️
Tip

Copy/Paste Tip

  1. Use the copy buttons on this dashboard.
  2. Click inside the Zoom chat box.
  3. Press Ctrl + V on Windows or Command + V on Mac.
  4. Press Enter to send it.

If highlighting text feels tricky, you can also right-click highlighted text and choose Copy, then right-click in Zoom chat and choose Paste.

⚠️
Note

Zoom Chat Paste Limit Note

Zoom chat does not always reliably accept long copied-and-pasted blocks of text. If a message is too long, Zoom may fail to send it or cut it off.

To avoid this, the announcements are split into two smaller copy/paste sections. Paste Announcement Part 1 first. Then paste Announcement Part 2.

Do we have any AA-related announcements?

This is a reminder that we always need leaders for our meetings. This is a service opportunity open to all. Please consider signing up to lead a future meeting. You can sign up at the link posted in chat. If you would like to be on our Google group, send a request to Robert. 7. Anniversaries and Milestones

Is anyone celebrating an AA Anniversary, a milestone of days, weeks, months, or years of recovery?

💬
Say

8. Ask for a Topic

We are now looking for a topic for the meeting related to recovery, something affecting your serenity or sobriety, today’s reading, or whatever’s on your heart that you would like to share.

Just a reminder, if you comment on another share, please be mindful that we share our own personal experiences and avoid giving direct advice to others when we share or message others in chat. Be mindful of the length of your share so that all who wish have an opportunity to share.

The floor is now open for discussion.

💬
Say

10. Close at 8:30 AM

At the end of the meeting – 8:30 am: Before we close, a reminder that this meeting will stay open for fellowship. Feel free to stick around if you’d like to chat. Let’s close with the we-version of the Serenity Prayer.

Closing Serenity Prayer, We Form
God, grant us the serenity to accept the things we cannot change,
the courage to change the things we can,
and the wisdom to know the difference.

🧑‍💻

If you are also the host and need to leave, assign another host before leaving so fellowship can continue.

Original host reminder: Please note if you are also the host, designate another host at the end of the meeting to keep the meeting room open for fellowship at the end of the meeting, if you need to leave.

Zoom Help

Zoom Help for Leaders

Claiming Host
  • To claim host, select Participants at the bottom of your Zoom screen, next to Chat.
  • Select Claim host.
  • You will need the host passcode. Contact someone who has led before if you need it.
Sharing Your Screen
  • Open the page you want to share before you try to share your screen.
  • In Zoom, select the green Share button.
  • Choose the correct page or window.
  • Select Share.
  • The window needs to be open in the background. It cannot be minimized.
  • When done, select Stop Share in the red box at the top of your screen.
Copying and Pasting Into Zoom Chat
  • Copy only one announcement section at a time.
  • Paste Announcements Part 1 first.
  • Then paste Announcements Part 2.
  • If Zoom does not send the message, split the section into smaller pieces and try again.
